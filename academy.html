Youth Academy

About the Academy:

The Youth Academy at Smith County Soccer Academy is a six-week soccer training experience tailored to young athletes between the ages of 5 and 11. Our program focuses on skill development, fostering a love for the sport, and promoting positive character traits both on and off the field.

Parents can choose from flexible training options that suit their family's schedule. Sessions are held twice a week, with options available on either Monday/Wednesday or Tuesday/Thursday (or both!).
