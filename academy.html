Smith County Soccer Academy

About the Academy

The Smith County Soccer Academy provides soccer focused training and development while having a fun and educational experience, allowing the player to get a head start in the game.

Our philosophy is to share a lifelong passion for the game, developing good habits on and off the field by inspiring each player to their full potential.

Smith County Soccer Academy is a private youth soccer academy comprised of five divisions: U5 coed, U7 coed, U9 coed, U11 coed, U13 coed. Players participate in weekly practices and scrimmages. Practices are run academy style. Players will be required to practice once per week, however we recommend and offer players at least two practice days per week. Teams will be formed and organized through the academy. Practices are offered Monday - Friday at 4:00 and 5:00 pm, registration is required for a specifit practice date and time before the session begins. Practice times are subject to availability.

If you have an existing team looking to join the Smith County Soccer Academy, please contact us to reserve a team, and group practice session.
