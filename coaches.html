Meet our staff

Erling Johnsen

Hey soccer fans!

I am Coach Erling Johnsen, and I am driven by a passion for soccer and a joy in sharing that passion with everyone. With a background deeply rooted in the beautiful game, I bring over five years of coaching experience to Smith County Soccer Academy.

My Journey:

My journey in soccer began as a player where I discovered the joy of the beautiful game. Transitioning to coaching allowed me to channel that passion into guiding young players towards their soccer ambitions.

Coaching Philosophy:

At the heart of my coaching philosophy is a belief in the holistic development of athletes. Beyond honing technical skills, athletic prowess and tactical understanding, I am dedicated to instilling values such as respect, integrity, ambition, and the importance of putting in genuine effort both on and off the field.

Qualifications:

U.S. Soccer Coaching License

Boulder County Soccer Club Academy Coach

Colorado Rapids Youth Program Coach

What Sets My Coaching Apart:

While I embrace the concept of learning to crawl before you walk, my approach goes beyond traditional training methods and challenges the player in an environment with no fear of failure. Each session is meticulously crafted to not only challenge athletic development and technical ability but also to stimulate cerebral capacity and hone problem-solving skills. Moreover, I place a strong emphasis on the value of education as an additional training mechanism, cultivating focus and cognitive abilities which I believe translate on the pitch. Another cornerstone of my coaching philosophy is the significance of recoveryâ€”a facet I believe is as crucial as the workout itself. Recovery encompasses mindful nutrition choices, prioritizing quality sleep for effective body rebuilding, and incorporating active recovery and cross-training methods. By integrating these elements, my coaching aims to provide a comprehensive and holistic approach to player development, both on and off the field.

Join Us on the Soccer Journey:

Whether an athlete is taking their first steps in soccer or aiming for higher levels of competition, I am committed to providing guidance, encouragement, and a platform for growth. Let's embark on this soccer journey together and create lasting memories on the pitch.

I look forward to being a part of the Smith County Soccer Academy family and helping each player reach their full potential.

See you on the pitch!
