About

Smith County Soccer Academy was created to provide the community a quality and fun way to experience soccer.

Youth programs include Soccer Start for the youngest age groups 3-6, the Academy training serves ages 5-11, and Youth Soccer League provides league play opportunities for academy students.

Adult programs include League play, pick-up games, and other fitness related activities.
