About Us

At Smith County Soccer Academy, we are passionate about the beautiful game and dedicated to providing the community with a quality and enjoyable soccer experience. Our academy was founded with a vision to nurture and develop young players, instilling important values both on and off the field.

We cater to players of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're just starting your soccer journey or looking to take your game to the next level, we have programs tailored to meet your needs. From recreational leagues and skill clinics to competitive teams and specialized training, our comprehensive offerings ensure that every player can find their place within our academy.

Our experienced and passionate coaching staff, led by Head Coach Erling Johnsen, brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to each training session and game. We strive to create a positive and supportive environment where players can thrive, make friends, and create lasting memories.

We invite you to explore our website and discover the range of programs and services we offer. Join us at Smith County Soccer Academy and become part of our passionate soccer community. We look forward to welcoming you and being a part of your soccer journey.
