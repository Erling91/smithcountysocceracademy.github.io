Adult Pickup Games

Join our vibrant community of soccer enthusiasts and enjoy friendly pickup games in a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere. Whether you're a seasoned player or just looking to have fun and stay active, our adult pickup games are open to players of all skill levels. Purely for enjoyment with an extra emphasis on not injuring anyone! Including yourself so warm up well.

Location and Schedule

Our pickup games take place at the Smith County Soccer Academy field located at:

123 Main Street
Smith County, State
ZIP Code

The pickup games are organized on the following days:

  • Mondays: 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Wednesdays: 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Fridays: 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Requirements

To participate in the adult pickup games, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • $3 (cash or venmo @SmithCountySoccer)
  • Bring your own soccer equipment, including appropriate shoes and shin guards
  • Sign the liability waiver before your first participation

Join Us

No registration is required to join our adult pickup games. Simply show up at the designated field during the scheduled times, check in, and pay, then get ready to have fun!
