Pickup Soccer Rules

Our pickup soccer games follow a set of rules to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all participants. Please familiarize yourself with the following rules before joining the games:

1. Fair Play

Respect your fellow players, opponents, and the game itself. Play with integrity, honesty, and good sportsmanship.

2. Safety First

Wear appropriate gear, such as shin guards and soccer shoes. Avoid dangerous tackles and excessive physical contact.

3. Teamwork

Communicate and collaborate with your teammates. Encourage a positive and inclusive atmosphere where everyone gets a chance to participate.

4. Offside Rule

Offside rule may or may not be enforced, depending on the agreement among players. Clarify the offside rule before the game starts.

5. Referee

In the absence of an official referee, players are expected to self-officiate. Resolve disputes through friendly discussions and fair play.

6. Equipment

Bring your own equipment, including a proper soccer ball and water bottle. Ensure the playing area is clear of any hazards before starting the game.

7. Game Duration

Game duration may vary depending on available time and agreement among players. Typically, games are played in two halves, with a short break in between.

8. Enjoyment

Above all, enjoy the game! Have fun, meet new people, and appreciate the joy of playing soccer in a friendly and relaxed environment.
