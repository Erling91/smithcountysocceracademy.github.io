About Our Sunday Showcase

Welcome to Smith County Soccer Academy's Sunday Showcase! We invite you to join us for a day of free soccer sessions that will give you a taste of the exciting training experiences we offer. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting your soccer journey, our showcase is designed to provide a fun and informative experience for everyone.

Event Details:

  • Date: 9/10
  • Location: Rose City Soccer Complex
  • Address: 10340 CR 490, Tyler TX, 75706
  • Times:
    • U5 (ages 4-5) @ 4:00pm
    • U7 (ages 5-7) @ 5:00pm
    • U9 (ages 6-9) @ 6:00pm
    • U11 (ages 7-11) @ 7:00pm
  • Registration Assistance: Interested in our programs? We'll help you with the registration process.
  • Free Giveaways: As a token of our appreciation for your interest, we have some exciting competitions with prizes for our participants.

Join us for this special event, and experience our academy style training session.
