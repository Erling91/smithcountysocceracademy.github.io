Smith County Soccer Club

Soccer Start

About Soccer Start

This program introduces soccer through unique challenges, games, and exercises while focusing on the all around development of the kids. At each level, children will learn and develop their soccer abilities. Classes will focus on FUNdamental agility, balance, coordination and soccer skills including passing, dribbling and shooting.

The age appropriate class descriptions can be found here. Class times vary throughout the week and the fee is $120 for the 6-week sessions. If you happen to be registering in the middle of a current season, no problem, we will prorate your registration to the end of the session.

Class Descriptions

Foals (2 - 3 year olds)

The class is focused on having fun, basic motor skills, introductory soccer skills and following directions. Adult / Parent participation is required for this class.

Colts (3 - 4 year olds)

This class is focused on having fun while incorporating additional soccer skills, balance, coordination, listening, following instructions and movement. It is a very active and engaging environment for kids to play and learn the game of soccer. Adult / Parent participation is not required unless needed on an individual bases.

Mustangs (4 - 5 year olds)

Classes will be filled with games and drills that are fun and soccer-centric. Children will be improving their motor skills, teamwork, and following instructions. We will introduce the “one ball concept” in order to prepare each player for more soccer game related activities. Each practice ends with a small sided scrimmage.

Stallions (5 - 6 year olds)

Children will play more challenging skill games and drills to further their soccer development. The emphasis is still on having fun as kids learn to improve their soccer touch, motor skills, teamwork, and following instructions. This class will prepare each player for the “real soccer game”. Each practice ends with a small sided scrimmage.

Smith County Soccer Academy

Once your player grows out of Soccer Start, it’s time to try the alternative in youth soccer. All paid Coaches. No Volunteers. All practices and games focus on the individual player.
