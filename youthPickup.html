About Youth Pickup Five-a-Side

At Smith County Soccer Academy, we believe in fostering a love for the game from an early age. Our Youth Pickup Five-a-Side program is designed to provide young players with a fun and engaging soccer experience. These 5-a-side pickup games are tailored to enhance skills, encourage teamwork, and create a passion for the beautiful game.

Our program focuses on creating a dynamic and inclusive environment where players can express themselves on the field, learn the fundamentals of soccer, and build lasting friendships. The 5-a-side format allows for more touches on the ball, increased decision-making opportunities, and a faster-paced, exciting gameplay that keeps the players actively involved.

Whether your child is a beginner or has some soccer experience, our Youth Pickup Five-a-Side program provides a platform for skill development, tactical understanding, and, most importantly, enjoyment. Join us and let your child experience the joy of playing soccer in a supportive and encouraging community.
